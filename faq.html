Frequently Asked Questions

People often need clarification, were always here to answer your questions


How much does it cost to join? It's free, we just ask you to bring your own water and wear appropriate clothing

Where do we run? We run everywhere, from the Downs to Harbourside, from Ashton Court to the City Centre, we love exploring Bristol.

When do we run? We usually run on Monday, Wednesday and Friday evenings, but we also organise weekend runs and special events

How long are the runs? Our runs are usually between 5km and 10km, but we can adjust the distance based on the group's preference and fitness levels

Who can join? Anyone can join, some persons have different mobility and need different pace so we go on flat runs (Harbourside) on Friday and Sunday to accommodate for them
